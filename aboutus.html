About us

About Company

Ucleve Traders is a smart and trusted scrap trading company founded by Anuj kumar, dedicated to making recycling simple, convenient, and eco-friendly. Our journey began with a goal to make scrap collection fair, easy, and sustainable. From a small start, we have grown into a reliable scrap buyer, serving homes, offices, and industries across the city. We buy and collect all kinds of scrap from homes, offices, and industries, offering fair prices, doorstep pickup, and instant payment while promoting a cleaner, greener environment.

Our Green Impact

At Ucleve Traders, we turn scrap into sustainable solutions, giving old materials a new life and reducing landfill waste. By recycling metals, electronics, wood, and more, we help create a cleaner city and a greener future. Every piece of scrap we collect contributes to protecting the environment, saving natural resources, and promoting eco-friendly practices that make a real difference for our community and the planet.

Our Services

Ucleve Traders offers a wide range of scrap collection and recycling services. We collect metal scrap, electronic scrap, office scrap, industrial scrap, battery scrap, solar scrap, and demolition scrap from homes, offices, and industries. Our team also provides dismantling services, office buyback solutions, industrial scrap management, and eco-friendly recycling. We ensure fair prices, doorstep pickup, and instant payment, making scrap recycling easy, convenient, and sustainable for everyone.
